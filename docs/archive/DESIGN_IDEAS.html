Mortar and Pistol

Medieval fantasy bullet heaven — magic-powered guns, alchemical weapons, elemental affinities.

A vampire-survivors-style bullet heaven where magic comes from guns, not wands. Every weapon is a purpose-built magical firearm — mortars loaded with potion shells, rifles etched with arcane formulas, pistols with wind vents along the barrels. Pick a hero, grab weapons, attune to an element, and transmute your arsenal into something devastating.

Heroes

Each hero starts with a weapon, an elemental affinity, and a unique passive. All heroes can pick up any weapon during a run.

The Wizard
Mid-range precision

Weapon: Bolt Rifle

Element: Lightning

Passive: +10% fire rate

Enchanted long-barrel rifle. Arcane formulas etched along the barrel focus magic into a single devastating bolt that punches through anything in its path.

The Alchemist
AoE & debuffs

Weapon: Scatter Flask

Element: Poison

Passive: +12% AoE radius

Modified blunderbuss that fires unstable flasks. On impact, the flask shatters into volatile shrapnel that sprays in all directions. Hisses, bubbles, smells of sulphur.

The Bombardier
Heavy explosions

Weapon: Powder Keg

Element: Fire

Passive: +15% damage

Massive iron-banded mortar that drops primed explosive kegs. The fuse burns down, then everything nearby goes up. Half the character's size.

The Assassin
Speed & burst

Weapon: Twin Barrels

Element: Wind

Passive: +15% move speed

Dual compact pistols with wind vents along the barrels. Fires both barrels in rapid succession — one-two punch that drops targets before they react.

Character Weapons

Each hero starts with a unique weapon. These are exclusive — they don't appear in the level-up pool for other heroes. They scale to Lv5 but do not transmute.

Bolt Rifle

Wizard · PIERCING

Single fast bolt that pierces ALL enemies in a line. Long range, high damage, moderate rate. The precision sniper — one shot, one clean line of destruction.

20-40 dmg · 0.8-1.2/s · 3-7 pierce · 500px range

Scatter Flask

Alchemist · BURST

Flask hits the nearest enemy, then shatters into 4-8 shrapnel projectiles spraying outward from impact. Short range, chaotic, satisfying.

10 flask + 4×shrapnel dmg · 0.7-1.0/s · 250px range

Powder Keg

Bombardier · MINE

Drops an explosive barrel at YOUR position. 0.8s fuse, then massive detonation. Requires positioning — drop it in a crowd, then get out. High risk, high reward.

25-60 dmg · 0.5-0.7/s · 90px radius · 0.8s fuse

Twin Barrels

Assassin · BURST-FIRE

Fires 2 rapid shots (0.1s apart) at the nearest enemy. Fast projectiles, quick cooldown. The dual-pistol double-tap — reliable, clean, satisfying.

10-22 ×2 dmg · 1.2-1.8 bursts/s · 300px range
